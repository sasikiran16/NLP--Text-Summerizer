Text Pre-Processing

To preprocess your text simply means to bring your text into a form that is predictable and analyzable for your task.
1). Converting text to lowercase (eg: "I AM CHOEZIN")
2). Sentence tokenize(eg: "I'm Choezin. This is a test.")
3). Word tokenize (eg: "I AM CHOEZIN")
4). Spell Check(eg: "Thss is a test")
5). Stemming(eg: "test functioning")
6). Remove Tags (eg: "this <\test> tag")
7). Remove numbers (eg: "test 1243")
8). Remove punctuation (eg: "test this! or $")
